Our Story

The owner Victoria the owner and her employees strives to make tasty food from scratch. Including fresh breads, biscuits, treats, soups and sandwiches. a little background story on our owner victoria. Victoria has a passion for food. Ben has a passion for Victoria’s passions. Victoria was born and raised in NW Arkansas and attended college in Springfield, Missouri, where her passion for customer service began. From there, a burning desire for knowledge, specifically within quality food services, blossomed. Ben was born in Spokane, Washington and moved back and forth between Northern Nevada and Spokane during his childhood. He joined the Marine Corps as an infantryman during OEF/OIF and served 12 years before being medically retired. Ben holds two master’s degrees in various fields of psychology, but enjoys spending most of his time home with his kids, while working towards Victoria’s goals. Ben and Victoria met in Missouri at a restaurant where Victoria was working her way through college. Their first child was born in 2011, Aurora, and they finally tied the knot in 2015 on the farm that Ben spent every summer on. The family home, running three generations strong and raising the fourth in, has been a project home and the focus of many energies over the past four years. In January 2019 baby Denali was born, completing their perfect little family. Here, the focus turned to plunging feet first into making Victoria’s lifelong dreams a reality. And here we go...
